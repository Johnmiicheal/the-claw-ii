## ğŸ¯ How to Play

1. **Allow Camera Access** - The VHS camcorder needs to see you!
2. **Enter Your Name** - Make it memorable for the leaderboard
3. **Show Your Hand** - Move LEFT/RIGHT to control the claw
4. **Pinch to Grab** - Make a pinching gesture to snatch those gifts
5. **Drop in the Chute** - Release over the green chute to score
6. **Save Christmas** - Get 500 points (5 gifts) before time runs out!
